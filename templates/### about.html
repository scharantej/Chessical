About

This chess application is designed to provide a fun and challenging experience for players of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to improve your game or an experienced player seeking a worthy opponent, this app has something to offer.

The app features a variety of difficulty levels, allowing players to select a computer opponent that matches their skill level. It also includes an analysis feature that provides feedback on your moves and suggests ways to improve your strategy.

We hope you enjoy using this chess application!
