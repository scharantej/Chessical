Help

This section provides instructions on how to use the chess application.

Selecting a Computer Opponent:

  1. On the homepage, select the desired rating for the computer opponent from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click the "Start Game" button to begin the game.

Making Moves:

  1. Click on a square to select a piece.
  2. Click on another square to move the piece to that square.
  3. The computer will respond with its move.

Getting Analysis:

  1. Click the "Analyze" button to receive analysis of the current game state.
  2. The analysis will include a score for the current position and a suggested best move.
